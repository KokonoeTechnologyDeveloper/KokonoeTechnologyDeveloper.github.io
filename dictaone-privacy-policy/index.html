Privacy Policy for DictaOne

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Version: 1.0

Privacy Summary

We prioritize your privacy:

  • No account creation required
  • All dictation content and practice records stay on your device only
  • We don't access or store your text scanning images
  • Payments are handled directly by Apple/Google
  • You can use our app without providing any personal information
  • Children can use the app under parental supervision

Technology Company Limited ("we," "our," "us") created DictaOne as a multi-language self-dictation mobile application. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Minimal Data Collection Principle

We are committed to minimal data collection:

  • We do not require account creation
  • We do not collect email addresses or personal identifiers
  • All dictation content and practice records are stored locally on your device
  • We only collect anonymous analytics data for app improvement
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple/Google

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use DictaOne, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: Features used, session duration, app performance data (anonymous)
  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction data, advertising ID for personalized ads
  • Crash Reports: Technical data for app stability and performance improvement
  • General Location: Based on IP address for service localization

2.2 Local Data Storage

All your dictation texts, practice records, and settings are stored locally on your device:

  • We do not have access to your dictation content
  • Password-protected content is encrypted on your device
  • Captured images for text scanning are processed locally
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data

2.3 In-App Purchases

  • Purchase transactions are processed directly by Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • We receive only purchase confirmation from the app stores
  • We do not handle payment information directly

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing app functionality
  • Processing in-app purchases through App Store platforms
  • Displaying relevant advertisements
  • Analyzing app usage to improve features
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. App-Specific Features and Privacy

4.1 Text Scanning and Processing

  • Images are processed locally using Google ML Kit on your device
  • We do not store or transmit scanned images to our servers
  • Text extracted from images is stored only on your device

4.2 Multi-language Support

  • Language preferences are stored locally on your device
  • UI language settings do not leave your device

4.3 Password Protection

  • Passwords for hidden texts are stored locally using device encryption
  • We cannot recover forgotten passwords
  • Passwords are never transmitted to our servers

4.4 Dictation Practice

  • All practice history remains on your device
  • Progress tracking is entirely local
  • We do not have access to your practice performance

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Advertising

We use Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and process certain device information for advertising purposes. You can opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Turn off "Personalized Ads"
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

5.2 Other Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services: For app functionality
  • Google ML Kit: For on-device text recognition
  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics

Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

6. Your Privacy Rights

While we collect minimal information (device identifiers, advertising IDs, and anonymous analytics), you still have privacy rights:

  • Opt-out: Disable personalized ads in your device settings
  • Access: Request what limited data we have about your device
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your advertising ID from our analytics

For EU/EEA residents (GDPR) and California residents (CCPA/CPRA), additional rights apply. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Since your dictation content is stored only on your device, you have full control - simply delete the app to remove all local data.

7. Data Retention

  • Device Information: While app is installed
  • Usage Analytics: 90 days
  • Advertising Data: 30 days
  • Local App Data: Until app is uninstalled

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for educational purposes and may be used by children under parental supervision:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do not display behaviorally targeted ads to users we know are under 13
  • All dictation content created by children remains on the device
  • Parents have full control over their child's data through device management

9. International Data Transfers

Your limited analytics data may be transferred internationally with appropriate safeguards. Your dictation content always remains on your device.

10. Data Security

  • All personal dictation content is stored locally on your device
  • Password-protected content uses device encryption
  • We implement industry-standard security for the limited data we collect
  • You are responsible for your device security

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices:

Technology Company Limited
Email: support@kidstart.com
