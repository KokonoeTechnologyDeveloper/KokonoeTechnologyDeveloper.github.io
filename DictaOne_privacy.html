Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the DictaOne app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Technology Company Limited (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application is a text dictation tool that processes and stores all data locally on your device. The Application:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not transmit your text input to any servers
  • Does not transmit captured images or scanned text to any servers
  • Stores all data locally on your device only
  • Does not track usage or analytics

The Application features include:

  • Text processing and dictation practice
  • Text scanning and recognition from images
  • Password protection for hidden text
  • Multi-language support
  • Local storage of dictation materials

All these features operate locally on your device without data transmission.

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Camera Access

The Application requests camera access solely for text scanning functionality. Camera usage:

  • Images are processed locally on your device for text recognition
  • No images are transmitted to external servers
  • Camera data is not stored permanently unless explicitly saved by the user
  • Text recognition is performed using on-device Google ML Kit services

Ad Support

The Application uses Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and process certain device information and usage data for advertising purposes. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

  • All text input is stored locally on your device only
  • Captured images for text scanning are processed locally and can be deleted
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • The Service Provider has no access to your stored data
  • Users are responsible for their own data backup
  • Data can be permanently deleted by uninstalling the Application
  • Password-protected content remains local to your device
  • Dictation materials and practice records are stored locally
  • Language settings and preferences are stored on device only
  • No cloud backup is provided by the Application

Third Party Access

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to aid the Service Provider in improving the Application and their service. The Service Provider may share your information with third parties in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

Please note that the Application utilizes third-party services that have their own Privacy Policy about handling data. Below are the links to the Privacy Policy of the third-party service providers used by the Application:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google ML Kit (for text recognition)

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all use of the Application by uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstall processes available on your mobile device or via the Google Play store.

Children

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. Since all data is stored locally, parents can control app usage through standard device controls.

Security

Since all data is processed and stored locally on your device:

  • Data security depends on your device's security settings
  • Camera data is processed locally for text recognition
  • No server-side security is needed as no data is transmitted
  • Users are responsible for their device security
  • Users should regularly backup important data
  • Password protection security depends on user-chosen passwords
  • Users are responsible for remembering their passwords as they cannot be recovered

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes to the Privacy Policy by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

This privacy policy is effective as of 2025-06-11

Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to:

  • Local storage of your text input on your device
  • Camera access for text scanning functionality
  • Local processing of captured images for text recognition
  • Local storage of dictation materials and practice records
  • Viewing of advertisements through Google AdMob
  • The terms outlined in this Privacy Policy

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about the practices, please contact the Service Provider via email at support@kidstart.com.
