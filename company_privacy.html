Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2025
Last Updated: June 25, 2025

Technology Company Limited ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile applications collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

Our applications are designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information like names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Text content you create or scan in our apps
  • Photos or images beyond temporary processing
  • Location data beyond general country/region
  • Contact lists or device files

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

Our apps and advertising partners may automatically collect limited technical information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Advertising Data: Advertising ID, general location (country/region), app usage patterns for ad personalization
  • Usage Analytics: App crashes, performance data, feature usage (anonymized)
  • Purchase Information: Transaction details processed by Google Play Store

2. How We Use Information

The limited information collected is used to:

  • Show Relevant Ads: Display personalized advertisements through Google Ads
  • Process Payments: Handle in-app purchases through Google Play billing
  • Improve Our Apps: Fix bugs, enhance features, and optimize performance
  • Prevent Fraud: Detect unusual activity and protect against misuse

3. Information Sharing

We share information only with essential service providers:

  • Google (Ads & Play Store): For advertising and payment processing
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Advertising

Our apps display advertisements provided by Google Ads. Google may:

  • Use your device's advertising ID to show personalized ads
  • Collect information about which ads you view or tap
  • Use this information to improve ad targeting across apps and websites

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings or through Google's ad settings.

5. Your Privacy Rights

For All Users

Regardless of your location, you can:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Ask questions about our privacy practices

5.1 European Union (GDPR) Rights

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights:

  • Access: Request information about data we have about you
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Complain: Lodge complaints with your local data protection authority

5.2 California (CCPA/CPRA) Rights

California residents have the right to:

  • Know: What personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-Discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights

6. Data Security

We protect your information through:

  • Secure data transmission (encryption)
  • Limited data collection and storage
  • Regular security updates
  • Restricted access to any collected data

7. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary:

  • App Usage Data: 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Advertising Data: As determined by Google Ads policies
  • Purchase Records: As required by app store policies
  • User Requests: Deleted promptly upon verified request

8. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

Our apps are available globally. Information may be processed in various countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

10. Third-Party Services

Our apps use these third-party services:

  • Google Ads: Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services: Google Privacy Policy

11. Do Not Track

Our apps do not respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals, but you can control ad personalization through your device's privacy settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do:

  • We'll post the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Significant changes will be highlighted in the app
  • Continued use of our apps means you accept the updated policy

13. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your privacy rights:

Technology Company Limited
Email: privacy@kidstart.com

Response Times:

  • General inquiries: 5 business days
  • Privacy rights requests: 30 days
  • California residents: 45 days
