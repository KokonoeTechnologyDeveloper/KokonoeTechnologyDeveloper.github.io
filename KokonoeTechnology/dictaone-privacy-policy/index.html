Privacy Policy for DictaOne

Effective Date: June 30, 2025

Last Updated: June 30, 2025

Version: 1.0

Privacy Summary

Your Privacy at a Glance:

  • ✓ No account creation required
  • ✓ All dictation content stays on your device only
  • ✓ We only collect advertising data (with your choice)
  • ✓ Payments handled by Apple/Google
  • ✓ Child-safe with parental controls

Kokonoe Technology Company Limited ("we," "our," "us") created DictaOne as a multi-language self-dictation mobile application. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Minimal Data Collection Principle

We collect almost no data:

  • No account creation required
  • No email addresses or personal identifiers
  • All dictation content stored locally on your device only
  • No usage analytics or app performance data
  • Payment processing handled entirely by Apple/Google

Legal Basis for Any Data Processing (GDPR):

  • Consent: For personalized advertising (when you choose it)
  • Legitimate Interest: For basic app functionality
  • Legal Obligation: For compliance with laws

2. Information We Collect

2.1 The Only Data We Collect

When you choose personalized ads:

  • Advertising ID for relevant ad delivery (collected by Google AdMob)

For in-app purchases:

  • Purchase confirmation status from Apple/Google (we don't handle payment details)

That's it. We don't collect anything else.

2.2 What Stays on Your Device (We Can't Access)

  • All dictation texts and practice records
  • Password-protected content (encrypted locally)
  • Scanned images and extracted text
  • Language preferences and settings
  • Progress tracking and performance data

Important: Uninstalling the app deletes all your local data.

2.3 Your Ad Choice

First time using the app:

  • "Accept Personalized Ads" - We collect advertising ID for relevant ads
  • "Basic Ads Only" - Generic ads, no data collected
  • "Go Ad-Free" - Purchase removes all ads

Change anytime: App Settings or device ad controls.

3. How We Use Information

We use the limited data only for:

  • Providing app functionality
  • Displaying ads (when you consent)
  • Processing purchase confirmations
  • Complying with legal obligations

We never:

  • Analyze your dictation content
  • Track your learning progress
  • Create detailed user profiles
  • Share data with others (except AdMob when you consent)

Delete all app data: Uninstall the app from your device

4. App Features and Privacy

Text Scanning: Google ML Kit processes images locally on your device. Images never leave your device.

Password Protection: Passwords encrypted locally using device security. We can't recover forgotten passwords.

Multi-language: Language preferences stored locally only.

5. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (when personalized ads enabled):

  • Collects advertising ID for ad delivery
  • You can opt-out: iOS Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising (OFF) or Android Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out

App Stores (Apple/Google):

  • Handle all payment processing
  • We only receive purchase confirmation

Google ML Kit: Processes text scanning locally on your device.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Everyone:

  • Turn off personalized ads anytime
  • Delete app to remove all local content
  • Contact us about any data we hold

EU/EEA Residents (GDPR Articles 15-22):

  • Access, correct, or delete your data (Articles 15-17)
  • Object to processing or withdraw consent (Articles 21, 7)
  • Data portability where applicable (Article 20)
  • EU residents can file complaints with their national data protection authority
  • You can contact your local data protection authority if unsatisfied with our response
  • Advertising data retained for up to 26 months, deleted automatically

California Residents (CCPA):

In the past 12 months, we collected:

  • Advertising identifiers (when you chose personalized ads)
  • Purchase confirmation status (from app stores)

We do not sell personal information and have not sold personal information in the past 12 months.

  • Delete your data
  • Opt-out of personalized ads
  • Non-discrimination for exercising rights

To exercise rights: Email dpo.kokonoe.technology@artsnonet.com

7. Data Retention

We keep very little:

  • Advertising data: Up to 26 months (managed by Google AdMob)
  • Purchase status: Until you uninstall the app
  • Local content: Until you delete the app (we can't access this anyway)

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Age verification: Before using personalized ads, users must confirm they are 13 or older. Users under 13 automatically receive basic ads only and cannot enable personalized advertising.

Under 13 protections:

  • No personalized ads, basic ads only
  • Parental consent required
  • All educational content stays on device
  • Parents can contact us to delete any data

Parental rights: Review, delete, or control child's limited data by contacting us.

9. International Data Transfers

Your educational content: Never transferred - stays on your device.

Advertising data: May be transferred internationally with appropriate safeguards through Google's privacy framework.

10. Data Security

Your content: Encrypted locally on your device using industry-standard security.

Limited data we collect: Protected with appropriate technical and organizational measures.

Your responsibility: Keep your device secure and updated.

11. Legal Information

  • Governing Law: Hong Kong law, except where your local laws provide greater protection.
  • Severability: If any provision is unenforceable, the rest remains valid.
  • Assignment: You may not transfer your rights; we may assign with notice where required.
  • Disputes: Contact us first. Our liability limited to amount paid for app (if any).
  • Changes: We'll notify you of material changes through the app.

12. Contact Us

For privacy questions:

Email: dpo.kokonoe.technology@artsnonet.com
Company: Kokonoe Technology Company Limited
